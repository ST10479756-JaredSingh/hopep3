Our Community Projects

Raised vegetable and herb beds, cultivated by volunteers and shared by our neighborhood families.

Interactive workshops and school partnerships encouraging the next generation to grow their own food.

We’ve planted over 50 fruit trees — including mango, guava, and lemon — providing free produce to locals.

Turning organic waste into nutrient-rich compost that sustains our gardens and reduces landfill waste.

Collecting and storing rainwater helps us irrigate the garden sustainably during dry months.

Monthly workshops teach eco-friendly living — from recycling to solar energy and water conservation.

Partnering with local markets and residents, we donate fresh produce and pantry items to families in need.

Dedicated flower beds attract and protect pollinators — vital to the health of our entire ecosystem.
