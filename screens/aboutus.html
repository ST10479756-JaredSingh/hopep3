About Us

Founded in 2018, Hope Harvest Community Garden began as a small group of neighbors passionate about food security and sustainability. Over the years, we have grown into a thriving non-profit organization, providing fresh produce to low-income residents and empowering our community through education.

Our mission is to cultivate sustainable gardens that nourish both people and the planet. We aim to educate our community and reduce food insecurity one harvest at a time.
